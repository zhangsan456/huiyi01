Welcome to the official invitation of the 1st INTERNATIONAL SYMPOSIUM OF SPECIAL SHIPS AND INTELLIGENT SYSTEMS 2023, where new professionals converge to explore the theme of “SPECIAL SHIPS AND INTELLIGENT SYSTEMS”. This prestigious event will take place at Sanya, China, from 16th December to 18th December 2023.
The purpose of the conference
To create favourable conditions for the development and realisation of innovative abilities of professional and maritime industry enterprises, to engage them in active research and exploratory activities to participate in the solution of urgent scientific, humanitarian and scientific-technical problems.
Thematic areas of the conference (partially)
  • 1.Automation and engineering of special ships
  • 2.Intelligent systems in Navigation, Transport and Management Systems
  • 3.Automation and Computer Science Section on special ships
  • 4.Intelligent systems on Water transport operations
  • 5.VR and AR training methodology for marine transport specialists
